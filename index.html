About the Holy Journey

Char Dham Yatra is considered one of the most auspicious spiritual journeys in India. It starts from Haridwar/Rishikesh and covers the four high-altitude Himalayan shrines.

  • Yamunotri: Dedicated to Goddess Yamuna.
  • Gangotri: The origin of the holy river Ganga.
  • Kedarnath: One of the 12 Jyotirlingas of Lord Shiva.
  • Badrinath: Dedicated to Lord Vishnu.

Standard 10-Day Itinerary (Char Dham)

Day 1-2: Yamunotri Shrines

Arrival at Haridwar followed by a scenic drive to Barkot and the first holy darshan.

Day 3-4: Gangotri - Origin of Ganga

Journey through the lush valleys of Uttarkashi to the source of the holy river.

Day 5-7: Kedarnath Ji

The heart of the pilgrimage. A transformative experience at 11,000 feet.

Day 8-10: Badrinath & Return

Final darshan at the abode of Lord Vishnu and smooth return journey to Haridwar.
